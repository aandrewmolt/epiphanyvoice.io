FAQs

Q: What makes Epiphany different from other voice-based note-taking apps?

Epiphany stands out for its minimal friction. Unlike traditional note-taking apps, it eliminates unnecessary steps, allowing you to seamlessly capture ideas and return to what you were doing without the cost of context-switching.

Q: Can I use Epiphany for task management directly?

Epiphany is designed to integrate with task management tools like Asana and Notion, so the app is more geared towards efficiently capturing ideas and delegating tasks.

Q: Is Epiphany compatible with both iOS and Android?

Currently, Epiphany is available for iPhone and Apple Watch. The team is exploring options for expanding to Android in the future.

Q: How secure are my voice notes and transcriptions in Epiphany?

Epiphany prioritizes user privacy and employs encryption protocols. Your voice notes and transcriptions are secure and only accessible to you
